Fun Facts & Photos

  • The series consists of 10 seasons, with a total of 236 episodes.
  • Each episode is 22 minutes long. These were recorded in the Warner Bross. studio in California, with live audiences. About 300 people.
  • Friends aired Carol and Susan's first lesbian wedding. In some countries it was censored. A breakthrough for the time.
  • Matt LeBlanc had $11 in the bank when he auditioned for Joey.
  • Jennifer Aniston, in principle, was going to play Monica and Courtney Cox was going to be Rachel. They suggested that the producers swap roles.
  • Lisa Kudrow played Ursula Buffay before Phoebe in Mad About You, a highly successful series in the 90s.
  • The show's intro sequence, in which they dance and get wet in the fountain, was recorded at 4:00 a.m. at a fountain at the Warner Bros. Ranch.
  • The final chapter was recorded without an audience so that there would be no leak.
